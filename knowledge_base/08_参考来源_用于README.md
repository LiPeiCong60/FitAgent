4. International Society of Sports Nutrition Position Stand: protein and exercise.
5. Mifflin MD et al. A new predictive equation for resting energy expenditure in healthy individuals.
6. National Academies / NCBI Bookshelf. Description of the Acceptable Macronutrient Distribution Range.
7. Dietary Guidelines for Americans, 2020–2025.
8. CDC. Steps for Losing Weight.
9. NIH News in Health. Healthy Weight Control.
10. Mayo Clinic. Weight loss strategies and realistic goals.
